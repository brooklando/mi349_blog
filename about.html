About Anti-Spoil Analyzing

I've made this blog as a way to remind myself of the amazing games I've played throughout my life, but also to provide an unprofessional and honest opinion about these games. Why seek professional opinion when you can get the opinion of someone just as casual as you? (No offense). Anti-Spoil Average, as the title says, operates as a spoil-free source of gaming reviews from the average person. I hate spoilers just as much as you do, so you're safe with me (and my opinion, of course!)
